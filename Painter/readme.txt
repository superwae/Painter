simple paint project using openGl / GLFW
please download and link openGl and GLFW libraries

Controls 
1 2 3 to increase color (R G B)
4 5 6 to decrease color (R G B)
press Q then 4 points with the left mouse click to drow a square
press T then 3 points with the left mouse click to drow a rectangle
press L then 2 points with the left mouse click to drow a line
press P then 4 points with the left mouse click to drow a Point
press W then press as many points you want with the left mouse click then press mouse right click to drow poly
press C then 2 points one being the center of the circle and the other its edge to drow a circle
